This channel is currently in development. Backend downloading exists, but collection + compression does not. This service is available in the main branch.

Backend support is being worked on, but question creation currently does not work. This channel is supported on the main branch.
Nintendo Channel
While Disconnect24 has a plan of how to do this. The video format used, Mobiclip for Wii, has been recently figured out.

While Disconnect24 would like to support this in the future, no focus is being devoted on it at this time.
Wii no Ma
Disconnect24 is not officially supporting development on this channel.
Wii Speak Channel
Due to the high amount of work required and current priorities, Disconnect24 is not investigating this service right now. This channel is available on the main branch.
